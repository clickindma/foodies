About Us

Our Story

At Chaap E Punjab, we bring you the authentic flavors of North India with a touch of Chinese fusion. Our chefs carefully prepare each dish using traditional recipes and the freshest ingredients to create a memorable dining experience.

Specializing in tandoori chaap, kebabs, and a variety of vegetarian delicacies that mimic the taste and texture of non-vegetarian favorites, we've created a unique culinary experience that has won the hearts of food lovers in Kota.
